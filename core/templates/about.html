About Us

SEDS is an international non-profit organization to raise awareness and advocate young minds on space exploration and the space industry. It has international chapters all over the globe, SEDS INDIA being one among them which in itself has 9 local chapters nation-wide. Along with the rest of the collegiate chapters, our goal is to provide a premier platform for broadening one's knowledge by engaging in space-sciences and related fields and as well as act as a center for networking and and opportunities. To illuminate the spirit of love and enthusiasm for the space field, our members are encouraged to engage in various technical projects, engineering competitions, workshops, social events, seminars, outreach programs and space-related activities.
