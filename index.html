About Us

SEDS CUSAT provides a community where like minded enthusiasts are openly welcome to be a part of a big family. A family of space geeks who come together to engage, explore and share the fun out of love for the wonders of space. SEDS CUSAT facilitates and coordinates various technical projects, engineering competitions, workshops, social events, seminars, outreach programs and activities for our SpaceFam. This is where we take initiative to learn from each other to enhance our understanding of space-sciences and at the same time create memories to reminisce while gazing up at the stars and beyond!
