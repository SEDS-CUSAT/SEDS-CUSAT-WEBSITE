ABOUT SEDS CUSAT

SEDS CUSAT is part of SEDS India. SEDS India is the Indian wing of the largest space-related student body in the world. It is a premier platform for engaging in space-sciences and related fields, broadening one's knowledge, and gaining opportunities to make valuable connections. It's a platform for people to share their love and interest in the space field and network within the industries. Members are engaged in various technical projects, engineering competitions, workshops, social events, seminars, outreach programs and space-related activities. The goal is to promote space exploration and educate young minds in space science, technology and its benefits.
